The World of Urbex

I've always been fascinated by abandoned places, from buildings to towns. As I've expanded my research and exploring everything on the internet, I've learned the passion for abandoned places (or exploring them) is called Urban Exploring. Urban exploration, often shortened to UE or ubex, is the exploration of man-made structures, usually abandoned ruins or not usually seen components of the man-made environment. The typical focus for this hobby is photography and historical interest/documentation. Sometimes urban exploring can involve trespassing onto private propery, this isn't always the case.
