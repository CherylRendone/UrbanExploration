Rose Island

First on the list, Rose Island. Rose Island is available for the public to visit; it is located in Charlestown, Indiana in the state park and can be accessed by Trail #3. Witch's Castle

It is said the Witch's Castle used to be owned by three sisters who were said to be witches. James Noble Wood, the founder of Utica, Indiana. Wood is said to have claimed the land in compensation for his military service and evicted the sisters. The sisters were not happy about it, but were forced out of their home. In the 1950's it was rebuilt as a tourist home known as Mistletoe Falls. There ended up being an unexplained fire that burned the structure to the ground and the castle was abandoned. If you would like to read more about the past of the Witch's Castle, warning: there is claims of paranormal activities as well as the history having some pretty intense stories, you can find more here! More information can also be found by searching Witch's (or Witches) castle into Google.
